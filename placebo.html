El placebo es mío

Synopsis

Photographic personal project to express my concerns about the alienation caused by technologies. Although this is not a new issue, the raise of mobile devices has made the problem more obvious and dangerous for a society that's less and less in touch with reality.

My approach pretends to make the observer think about their own behaviours through the use of humour.

Introduction

I was raised in the late eighties in a house full of books in a small town in the mountains with a poor TV signal. This made little Angela read many books, play with cards, toys, paper and basically anything within reach of children. Having time to get bored helps building awareness and imagination.

The name "El placebo es mío" is a word game in Spanish to express the irony in the fact that people feel happy and proud of being absorbed by their lack of interest and culture.

The concept

Take people who have lost the inquisitiveness and portrait them with a tv as head.

The process

The elements that appear in the pictures are mock-ups or real objects. Except for the pidgeons, those were extracted from several pictures, taken one by one in the same location.

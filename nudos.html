Nudos y desnudos

Synopsis

Photographic personal project to illustrate my journey to self-knowledge. I use the self-portrait to create manipulations and body compositions that express the rollercoaster of emotions I go through while I grow as a person.

I try to express the everyday problems ordinary people face, and the emotions they create in us, as frustration, shame, sadness, loneliness, fear and uncertainity. This work has been showcased in magazines all over the world.

Skills

  • Photoshop
  • SLDR photography
  • Improvisation
  • Colour and composition
  • Brand identity

Concept

The name of the project "Nudos y desnudos" is a word game in Spanish, meaning "Knots and nudes" as the process allows me to tangle nudes and untangle (emotional) knots.

Process

This is an ongoing project, but for several months, I worked on this every day, polishing the process more and more.

In general I take several pictures in the same location with the same light, generally using my own body, as it's always available.
